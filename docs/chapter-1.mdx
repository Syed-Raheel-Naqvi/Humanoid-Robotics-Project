# Chapter 1: Fundamentals of Humanoid Robotics

This chapter introduces you to the fundamental concepts of humanoid robotics. You'll learn about the basic components that make up these sophisticated machines and how they work together to create human-like movements and behaviors.

## Key Topics in This Chapter

- What makes a robot "humanoid"
- Core components of humanoid robots
- Actuators and their role in movement
- Sensors for environmental awareness
- Control systems and algorithms

## Getting Started

Humanoid robots are among the most complex and fascinating types of robots due to their human-like form and potential for human-robot interaction. They differ from other robots in that they are designed to mimic the human body structure, typically featuring a head, torso, two arms, and two legs.
